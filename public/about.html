About Grover'z IELTS Preparation

Grover'z IELTS & Immigration Consultants is providing Overseas Resettlement Solutions. In just a short span of time, we have won the trust and respect among our clients. We are trusted name in the immigration field for providing worldwide immigration consultancy services.

Our counselors have strong expertise and extensive experience in handling Immigration Services for Canada, Australia, and England. Students who opt for overseas education has the highest opportunity for higher education and jobs in Australia, Canada, U.K., and U.S.A. We are associated with top universities, WHERE WE ASSURE ADMISSIONS AND GIVE VISA GUIDANCE TO THE STUDENTS.

We have changed the life of every student towards their career studies with expertise counselling and effective training to their higher education abroad depending on the academic credentials by way of professional counselling and evaluation of the students for their required specialization fields and research proposals for higher studies. Our Higher Education programmes are suited best for students pursuing their Under Graduates & Post Graduates studies.
